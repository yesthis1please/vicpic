VicPic iOS/iMessage Sticker Pack Privacy Policy

VicPic does not collect personally identifying information through its iMessage sticker pack. Apple collects some non-personal data about users of sticker packs, such as crash data, number of installations per geographic region, and iOS version/Apple devices used with the sticker pack app. See http://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ for more information on what is collected. This information is available to VicPic. VicPic cannot identify users through this information and does not share this information at all.
